Honey-Basted Roast Turkey

Description

Honey glazed turkey

seasoned with berries and stuffed with onions

Ingredients

  • 1 small turkey (10-12 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons of hazelnut or peanut oil
  • ½ tea spoon ground sage
  • 1/2 tea spoon ground dried spicebush berries or allspice
  • Salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 1 small bunch wild onions or green onions
  • 3-4 table spoons honey
  • A decorative string of cranberries and bay leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 Celsius (350 Fahrenheit)
  2. Remove giblets from turkey and reserve for gravy if desired
  3. Rinse turkey and pat dry
  4. Rub inside and out with oil and season with sage, spicebush berries, salt and pepper
  5. Stuff neck and body cavities with onions and truss, if desired
  6. Place turkey in roasting pan
  7. Roast, allowing 18-20 minutes per pound, until juices run clear with no hint of pink when thigh is pierced
  8. During the last hour of cooking, baste turkey 2 – 3 times with honey
  9. A good pan gravy can be made if you are pan roasting the turkey, though if you are spit roasting it you will not have the drippings
  10. Garnish with string of cranberries, if desired
