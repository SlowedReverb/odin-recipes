Navajo Peach crisp

Description

Peach cinnamon dessert

Ingredients

  • 6 large ripe peaches, peeled, pitted and sliced
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¾ cup unbleached flour
  • ¾ cup light brown sugar
  • ¼ tea spoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 table spoons pinon nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 Celsius (375 Fahrenheit)
  2. In a 1 ½ to 2 quart baking dish toss the peaches with granulated sugar and cinnamon
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, and salt
  4. Cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse meal
  5. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the peaches
  6. Sprinkle the top with nuts
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until golden brown on top
