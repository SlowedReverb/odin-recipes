Cranberry-Maple sauce

Description

Cranberry sauce with touches of maple syrup

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh cranberries
  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 1 cup water
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground dried spicebush berries or allspice (optional)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan combine cranberries, syrup and water
  2. Bring to boil over medium-high heat
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook, stirring often, for about 15 minutes, until berries have burst and sauce has thickened
  4. Stir in spicebush berry if desired
  5. Cool and serve at room temperature
  6. Sauce is best made several hours in advance so that flavors will blend
